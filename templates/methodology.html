In-season rankings

Weekly power rankings blend preseason combined rating with an algorithm margin rating built from completed FBS-vs-FBS games only. After the first FBS game, the blend is 90% preseason / 10% algorithm; preseason weight drops 10 points per additional FBS game until 10+ games, when rankings are 100% algorithm. Blending happens on the margin scale, not rank order.

Blended uses that fade rule. No Preseason shows the pure algorithm margin with no preseason component. Rank movement (▲/▼) compares each team’s rank to the prior week in the stacked weekly rankings file.

In-season projections

Each run models {{ meta.sim_count }} possible finishes to the {{ meta.season_year }} season from the current week forward. Completed games are pinned to their actual results in every simulation. Remaining games are simulated using per-team rating draws with uncertainty that shrinks as teams play more FBS games: σeff = 10 / (1 + √(FBS games played)).

Key metrics

Blended margin
Preseason rating and algorithm margin blended by games played (see above). Higher is better.
No Preseason
Algorithm-only margin rating from completed FBS-vs-FBS results.
Record
Overall W–L from completed games on the master schedule (all opponents).
Forecasted wins
Average regular-season wins across all {{ meta.sim_count }} in-season simulations.
SOS
Strength of schedule: average opponent rating across scheduled games with home/away/neutral site adjustments.
Playoff / Title odds
Chance of making the 12-team playoff or winning the national title from the current state of the season.

Playoff selection & bracket

Same 12-team rules as our preseason model: four power autobids, one Group of 5 autobid, remaining at-large spots by eligibility rules, then the official bracket format with byes for seeds 1–4.
